anther(dataRep)R Documentation

Embryogenic anthers.

Description

Anthers of the plant species Datura innoxia were prepared under different conditions and the number of embryogenic anthers counted.

Usage

data(anther)

Format

A dataframe with 4 variables.

y:
number of embryogentic anthers
n:
number of anthers prepared
storage:
storage conditions
force:
Centrifuging force

Source

References

Dobson, Annette, (2002), An Introduction to Generalized Linear Models, Chapma and Hall

Examples

data(anther)
