leafcalcium(dataRep)R Documentation

Calcium contents of four leaves four times measured

Description

For four leaves four times their calcium content was determined.

Usage

data(leafcalcium)

Format

A dataframe with 3 variables.

leaf
id of leaf
nr
id of measurement within leaf
ca
calcium measurment

Source

References

Examples

library(lattice)
data(leafcalcium)
dotplot(leaf~jitter(ca),groups=nr,data=leafcalcium,xlab='calcium')
