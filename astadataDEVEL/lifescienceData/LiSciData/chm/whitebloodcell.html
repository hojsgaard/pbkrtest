whitebloodcell(dataRep)R Documentation

Survival time of leukemia patients

Description

Survival time of leukemia patients in weeks after diagnosis

Usage

data(whitebloodcell)

Format

This data frame contains:

time:
survival time of patients after diagnosis in weeks
logcount:
log_10 (initial white blood cell count)

Source

Cox, D.R. and Snell, E.J. (1981) Applied Statistics: Principles and Examples, London: Chapman and Hall, Example U

References

Dobson, A. (2002) An introduction to Generalized Linear Models, London: Chapman & Hall, Exercise: 4.5-4.2

Examples

data(whitebloodcell)
