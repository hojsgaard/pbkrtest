suicideUSA(dataRep)R Documentation

Suicides in the USA in the years 1968-1970

Description

Suicides inm the USA

Usage

data(suicideUSA)

Format

A dataframe with 3 variables.

year:
year of suicide
month:
month of the year
n:
number of recorded suicides

Source

Haberman, S.J. (1978) Analysis of Qualitative Data, Vol. I, Introductory Topics, New York: Academic Press

References

Lindsey, J.K. (2002) Modelling Frequency and Count Data, Oxford: Claredon Press, Exercise 1.6(4)

Examples

data(suicideUSA)
attach(suicideUSA)
month<-ordered(month,levels=month[1:12])
w<-xtabs(n~month+year)
print(w)
days<-rep(31,12)
days[c(4,6,9,11,9)]<-30
days[2]<-28
plot(w[,1]/days,xlab='month',ylab='suicides per day',axes=FALSE)
axis(2)
axis(1,labels=levels(month),at=1:12,cex.axis=0.9)
box()
title('suicides in the USA in 1968')
