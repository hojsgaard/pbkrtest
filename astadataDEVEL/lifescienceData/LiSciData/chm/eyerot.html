eyerot(dataRep)R Documentation

eyerot

Description

In an experiment to control the attack of cereals by the fungus Pseudocercosporella her­potri­choides [dansk=knækkefodssyge] 10 treatments were tested in a complete block-trial with 4 replications. For treatments 2 to 10 pesticides of varying type and concentration were used where treatment 1 was the control treatment with no application of a pesticide.

From each parcel about 50 plants were taken the infestation of their stems were evaluated.

(knækkefodssyge =et snyltesvampe-angreb på planter, især kornarterne. Kendetegnet ved, at kun de nederste stængeldele og rodhalsen angribes. De vigtigste er gold-fodsyge, der fremkaldes af kulkernesvamp, angreb af hvededræbersvampen, og knækket fodsyge, forårsaget af øjepletsvampen. Either of two fungal diseases that affect grasses and are characterized by oval lesions.)

Usage

data(eyerot)

Format

This data frame contains:

treat:
treatment, 1= no application of pesticide, 2 to 10: treatments using pesticides
block:
block number, 1 to 4
score:
score giving the degree of infestation with fungus: 1= no infestation, 2=low (less than 30% of the circumference of a stem is infested, where infestation was strongest), 3= moderate (more the 50% of circumference is infested, tissue is fast), 4= the whole circumference is infested, tissue is decayed.
freq:
the number of animals found in each category

Details

log-linear model, ordinal respone, proportional odds

Source

Jørgensen, L. N. (1986) : Knækkefodsyge (Pseudocercosporella her­potri­choides) - typeopdeling og resistensundersrgelser for ben­zimidazoler og prochloraz. Nordisk Planteværnskonference 1986 : 93-102.

References

Søren Andersen (1989) Statistiske Analyse af Tælledata, Matematisk Institut, KVL p. 52, Kapitel 8, Eks. 1

Examples

data(eyerot)
tab<-xtabs(freq~treat+block+score,data=eyerot)
print(ftable(tab,row.vars=1))
