otitis(dataRep)R Documentation

Treatment of otitis

Description

Children who had one or both ears infected with otitis were either treated with the antibiotic cefaclor (CEF) or amoxicillin (AMO). After 14 days the state of their ears were reevaluated.

Usage

data(otitis)

Format

This data frame contains:

treat:
antibiotic treatment: CEF or AMO
entry:
number of ears with otitits at entry into the study
age:
age of child
nclear:
number of ears clear of acute otitis
nchild:
number of children

Source

Rosner (1989) Mutivariate methods for clustered binary data with more than one level of nesting JASA, 88, 373-380, Table 1.

References

Dobson, A. (2002) An introduction to Generalized Linear Models, London: Chapman & Hall, 2nd edition, Exercise 11.3, p. 211

Examples
