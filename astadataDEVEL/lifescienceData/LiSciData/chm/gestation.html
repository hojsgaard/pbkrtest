gestation(dataRep)R Documentation

Weight at gestational age of boys and girls

Description

Weight of boys and girls at different gestational (=under pregnancy, prenatal) ages.
Analysis of covariance

Usage

data(gestation)

Format

Data frame with four variables

boys.age:
age of boys [weeks]
boys.weight:
weight of boys [g]
girls.weight:
age of girls [weeks]
girls.weight:
weight of girls [g]

Details

Data are from the Table 2.2, page 22

Source

Dobson, A. (2002) An introduction to Generalized Linear Models, London: Chapman & Hall

Examples

data(gestation)
gestation<- reshape(gestation,direction='long',
          varying=list(age=c('boys.age','girls.age'),weight=c('boys.weight',
                                                              'girls.weight')),
          v.names=c('age','weight'),
          timevar='sex',times=c('boy','girl'))
summary(gestation)
