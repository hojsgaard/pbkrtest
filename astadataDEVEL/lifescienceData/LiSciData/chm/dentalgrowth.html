dentalgrowth(dataRep)R Documentation

Dentalgrowth in children

Description

In a longitudinal study the dental growth in children was analysed. The distance from the center of the pituitary gland to the maxillary fissure for children at ages 8, 10, 12 and 14 was measured.

Usage

data(dentalgrowth)

Format

A data frame with 4 variables.

age
age of child when the distance was measured
dist
distance
id
identification number of child whithin sex
sex
sex of child

Details

Source

Myers, H.R. , Montgomery D.C., Vining, G.G. (2002) Generalized Linear Models, Exercise 6.6

~~ possibly secondary sources and usages ~~

Examples

data(dentalgrowth)
library(lattice)
trellis.device('windows',theme='col.whitebg')
xyplot(dist~age|sex,group=id,data=dentalgrowth,type=c('p','l'))
