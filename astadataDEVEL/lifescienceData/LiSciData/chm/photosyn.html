photosyn(dataRep)R Documentation

Radiation and photosynthesis

Description

In a study the dependence of photosynthesis rate on radiation and water availability was examnined.

Usage

data(photosyn)

Format

Data frame containing

water:
level of water availability
radiation:
amount of radiation
rate:
rate of photosynthesis

Details

Source

Krzanowski, W (1998) An Introduction to Statistical Modelling. Arnold, London, p.107
