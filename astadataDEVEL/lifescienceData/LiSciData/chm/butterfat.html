butterfat(dataRep)R Documentation

butterfat

Description

Average butterfat percentage for random samples of mature cows (older than 5 years) and 2 year old cows from five different breeds.

Usage

data(butterfat)

Format

This data frame contains:

fat:
Butter fat percentage
age:
Age level: mature (> 5 years, 2-years: 2 years old)
breed:
Five breeds: Ayrshire, Canadian, Guernsey, Holstein-Fresian, Jersey

Details

ANOVA, 2-way

Source

Sokal, R. and Rohlf, F.J. (1981) Biometry, 2nd edition, p. 368.

Examples

      
data(butterfat)
