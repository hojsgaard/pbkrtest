enzyme(dataRep)R Documentation

velocity of encymatic reaction

Description

The velocity of an enzymatic reaction (measured as counts/min^2 of a radioactive reaction product) where obtained with and without the addition of puromycin.

Usage

data(enzyme)

Format

A dataframe with 3 variables.

treat:
'+': puromycin added, '-': not added
conc:
concentration of substrate [ppm]
v:
normal-bracket13bracket-normal
velocity of reation velocity of reation counts/min^2

Source

Bates, D.M. and Watts, D.G.(1988) Nonlinear Regression Analysis, Wiley: New York

References

Blæsild, P. (1996) Generaliserde Lineære Modeller og SAS, Matematisk Inbstitut, Aarhus Universitet

Examples

data(enzyme)
attach(enzyme)
plot(v~conc,subset=c(treat=='+'),pch='+')
points(v~conc,subset=c(treat=='-'))
legend(0.6,100,legend=c('with puromycin','without puromycin'),pch=c('+','-'))
title('velocity of encymatic reaction\n with and without puromycin')
