achievscore(dataRep)R Documentation

Achievement in training

Description

Experiment on the effect of three training methods
Analysis of Covariance

Usage

data(achievscore)

Format

Data frame containing

method:
Indicator for training method: A, B or C
y:
achievement score
x:
aptitude score, measured at the start of the experiment

Details

Data are orginally from Winer, B.J. (1971), Statistical Principles in Experimental Design, Second edition, McGraw Hill, New York

Source

Dobson, A. (2002) An introduction to Generalized Linear Models, London: Chapman & Hall, p. 106, table 6.12

Examples

data(achievscore)
attach(achievscore)
