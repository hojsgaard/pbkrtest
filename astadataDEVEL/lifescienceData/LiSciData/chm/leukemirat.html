leukemirat(dataRep)R Documentation

Leukemi treatments in rats

Description

In an experiment 30 rats had a leukemic condition induced. Three chemotherapy drugs were applied and as a response the number of cancer cell colonies was observed. As covariates the number of red and white blood cells were collected. Each animal was observed at 4 times.

Usage

data(leukemirat)

Format

A data frame with 6 variables.

ratid:
rat identification number
drug:
number identifying which drug used
time:
time period when measurements were taken
wbc:
count of white blood cells
rbc:
count of red blood cells
y:
count of cell colonies

Details

Source

Myers, H.R. , Montgomery D.C., Vining, G.G. (2002) Generalized Linear Models, Exercise 6.6

data(leukemirat)
leukemirat$drug<-factor(leukemirat$drug)

trellis.device('windows',theme='col.whitebg')
