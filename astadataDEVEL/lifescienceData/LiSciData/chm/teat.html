teat(dataRep)R Documentation

teat

Description

During a year the teat [dansk=patte] lesions in a dairy herd were registered.

Usage

data(teat)

Format

This data frame contains:

teat:
position of the teat on the udder [dansk= yver]: 1: right front, 2: right back, 3: left front, 4: left back
position:
position of the lesion: upper=upper part of the teat, lower=lower part of the teat
count:
number of teats

Details

log-linear model, two-ways table

Source

Agger, J. F. and Hesselholt, M. (1986) Epidemiology of teat leasions in a dairy herd, I: Description of incidence, location and clinical appearance, Nord. Vet.-Med. 38, 209-219

References

Søren Andersen (1989) Statistiske Analyse af Tælledata, Matematisk Institut, KVL p. 2, Eks. 3

Examples

data(teat)
