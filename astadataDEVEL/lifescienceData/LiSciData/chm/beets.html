beets(dataRep)R Documentation

Sugar beets data

Description

Yield of sugar beets as function of sowing and harvesting time in a split–plot experiment.

Usage

data(beets)

Format

A data frame with 30 observations on the following 5 variables.

harvest
Harvest time. A factor with levels harv1 harv2. The corresponding dates are 2/10 and 21/10.
block
The blocks, see details below.
sow
Sowing time. A factor with levels sow1 sow2 sow3 sow4 sow5. The corresponding dates are 4/4, 12/4, 21/4, 29/4 and 18/5
yield
Yield in Kg per 25 m2
sugpct
Sugar percentage

Details

The experiment was laid out as a as split plot experiment in blocks. There are three blocks each containing two whole–plot units corresponding to the harvest time. Each whole–plot unit is sub–divided into 5 split–plot units corresponding to the sowing dates.
