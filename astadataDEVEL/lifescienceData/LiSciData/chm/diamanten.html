diamonds(dataRep)R Documentation

Diamonds

Description

Characterisation of diamonds.

Usage

data(diamonds)

Format

This data frame contains:

lydefrihed:
degree of flawlessness; IFV: very very slightly imperfect; VS: very slighly imperfect
klarhed:
colour of diamond: E the lightest colour, H the darkest colour.
karat:
weight of the diamond in carat.
pris:
price of diamond in Singapore dollars
logpris:
log(price)

Details

The data are a reduction of the original data. Only diamonds of carat between 0.3 and 0.7 and colours E to H are considered. lydefrihed (in the original data called clarity) is condensed to IFV for catageories IF, VVS1 and VVS2, and VS subsumes VS1 and VS2.

Source

Singfat Chu (2001) Journal of Statistics education, Vol. 9,http://www.amstat.org/publications/JSE/jse_data_archive.html

References

Jens Ledet Jensen (2009) Et Nanokurs i Statistik, chapter 10.

Examples

      
data(diamonds)
