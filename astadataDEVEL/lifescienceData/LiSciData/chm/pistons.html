pistons(dataRep)R Documentation

Failure of piston rings

Description

These data concern the number of failures of piston rings for each of three legs of four steam-driven compressors.
ANOVA, Randomised complete block design, normal or poisson

Usage

data(pistons)

Format

Data frame containing

pistons:
Number of failures of piston rings
leg:
Position of leg (North=1, Centre=2, South=3)
compressor:
Compressor identification number

Source

Davies, O.L. and Goldsmith, P.L. (1972) Statistical Methods in Research and Production, 4th edn. Oliver and Boyd, Edinburgh, p. 324.
