fatherson(dataRep)R Documentation

Pearson's data on height of father and sons

Description

14 samples of a producte were randomly divided into 5 groups and each group was stored under different conditions. After storage the water content was measured.

Usage

data(fatherson)

Format

A dataframe with 2 variables.

father
height of father [inch]
son
height of son [inch]

Source

References

Examples

data(fatherson)
plot(father~son, data=fatherson)
