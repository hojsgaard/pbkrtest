dietsup(dataRep)R Documentation

Groweth of rats

Description

The growth rates of rats fed with different amounts of a diet supplement.
Sinmple linear rergression with quadratic repsonse curve

Usage

data(dietsup)

Format

Data frame with two variables

x:
amount of supplement [g]
y:
growth rate [coded units]

Details

Data are from the Table 14.10 page 480.

Source

Box, G. E. P., Hunter, W. G., Hunter, J. S. (1978) Statistics for Experiments

References

Examples

data(dietsup)
attach(dietsup)
plot(y~x)
fit<-predict(lm(y~x+I(x^2)))
lines(fit~x)
detach(dietsup)
