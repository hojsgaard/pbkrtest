From a ppduciton of flagstones with the fo

Experiment to analyse the influence of meachanical stress on the growth of soya bean plants. 52 seedlings were potted, one seedling per plot. The seedlings were randomly splitted up into four groups of 13 plants. In two groups the plants were shaken for 20 minutes daily. The groups that received the same amount of stress were grown under two different light conditions. After 16 days the plants were harvested and the total area of leaves for each plant was measured.

b:
Additive b: -1 not added, 1 : 10 kg of additive a added
