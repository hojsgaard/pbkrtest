heparinclott(dataRep)R Documentation

Clotting time of blood with heparin (HEPARINCLOTT)

Description

A standard preparation of heparin is compared to a test preparation by measuring the clotting time of blood for different doses of heparin. At each dose level two measurements are available.
normal, gamma with inverse link

Usage

data(heparinclott)

Format

A data frame with 20 observations and 3 variables.

time:
clotting time of blood (sec)
dose:
log-dose of heparin
preparation:
'standard' or 'test' preparation

Source

Osborn, J. F. (1979) Statistical Exercises in Medical Research, Blackwell, Oxford, p. 92, exercise 11.3.2

Examples

data(heparinclott)
