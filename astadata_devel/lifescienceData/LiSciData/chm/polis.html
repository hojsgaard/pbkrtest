polis(dataRep)R Documentation

Presence of Lizards on Island

Description

Subaset of data from a study on factors that influence the spider population on islands in the Gulf of Califonia. The present data give the presence/absence of the lizard (firben) uta on 19 islands in the Golf of California and the ratio of the perimeter to the area of the islands.

Usage

data(polis)

Format

This data frame contains:

island:
name of the island
paratio:
perimeter to area ratio of the islands
uta:
presence or absence of lizards (Uta) on the islands (P=present, A=absence)

Source

Polis, G.A., Hurd, S.D., Jackson, C.D. and Sanchez-Pinero, F. (1998) Multifactor population limitation: variable spatial and temporal control of spiders on Gulf of California islands, Ecology, 79, pp. 490–502

References

Quinn, Gerry. P. and Keough, Michael J. (2002) Experimental Design and Data Analysis for Biologists, University Press, Cambridge Box 13-1

Examples

data(polis)
