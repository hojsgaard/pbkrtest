soreThroat(dataRep)R Documentation

Post-operative sore thoat.

Description

Influcence of method of anaesthetic gas application. In a study the effect of two different airway-masks on the post-operative experience of sore throat was analysed. Data from surgeries with 10 consultants (anathesists) were collected on different patients.

Usage

data(soreThroat)

Format

A dataframe with 4 variables.

consultant
anaethesist
airway
type of aiway mas: FM: traditional face mask, LMA: laryngeal mask
y
patients with sore throat whithin 24 hours after operation
n
number of patients treated

Source

References

Collett, David (2003) Modelling Binary Data, Chapman and Hall, example 8.1, p. 278

Examples

data(soreThroat)
