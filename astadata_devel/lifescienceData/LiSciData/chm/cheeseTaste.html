cheeseTaste(dataRep)R Documentation

taste of four different cheese

Description

Persons were randomly assigned to four different types of cheeses and asked to characterize the taste on a scale from 1 to 9

Usage

data(cheeseTaste)

Format

A dataframe with 3 variables.

taste
response: evaluation of taste: from 1: strong dislike to 9: excellent taste
cheese:
type of cheese tasted
count:
number of answers

Source

McCullagh, P. and Nelder, J. A. (1989) Generalized Linear Models, London, U.K.: Chapman & Hall
