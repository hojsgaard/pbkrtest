activityLogger(dataRep)R Documentation

Daily activity of cows.

Description

Twenty loggers where placed on twenty cows and on three days the cows' daily activity was measured. A subset of the data activityCowsLong.

Usage

data(activityLogger)

Format

A dataframe with 4 variables.

logger:
id of logger
day1:
activity measurement for day 1
day2:
activity measurement for day 2
day3:
activity measurement for day 3
