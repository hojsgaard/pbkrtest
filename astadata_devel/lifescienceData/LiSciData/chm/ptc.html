ptc(dataRep)R Documentation

Taste threshold to phenylthiourea (PTC)

Description

For 104 medical students their taste threshold to phenylthiourea (PTC) was measured. Although threshold vary widely between idnividuals there seem to be two groups, taster and non-tasters.
Poisson

Usage

data(ptc)

Format

A data frame with 14 observations and 3 variables.

conc:
threshold of PTC [mg/l] at which studend tasted something
n:
number of students with the threshold
taster:
classifications as taster ('yes') or non-taster ('non')

Source

Osborn, J. F. (1979) Statistical Exercises in Medical Research, Blackwell, Oxford, p. 94, exercise 11.4.2

Examples

data(ptc)
