poisoned(dataRep)R Documentation

Survial of poisoned animals

Description

The survival of animals exposed to threee different poisons and four treatments is analysed.

Usage

data(poisoned)

Format

A data frame with 48 observations on 3 variables.

time
survival time [10hr]
treat
type of treatment: A, B, C or D
poison
type of poison: 1,2 or 3

Source

Box, G. E. P. and Cox, D. R. (1964), An Analysis of Transformations, Journal of the Royal Statistical Society, 211-243, discussion 244-252.

Examples

data(poisoned)
