clottingTime(dataRep)R Documentation

clotting time of blodd

Description

mean clotting times in [s] of blood for 9 and two lots of clotting agents

Usage

data(clottingTime)

Format

A dataframe with 3 variables.

conc:
plasma concentration
lot1:
clotting agent 1
lot2:
clotting agent 2

Source

Hurn, M.W., Barker, N.W. and Magath, T.D. (1945) The determination of prothrombin time following the administration of dicumarol with specific reference to thromboplastin, J. Lab Clin. Med., 30, 432-447

References

McCullagh, P. and Nelder, J. A. (1989) Generalized Linear Models, London, U.K.: Chapman & Hall, p. 300-302

Examples

data(clottingTime)
attach(clottingTime)
